John Michael Smith
Born: March 15, 1955
Died: December 10, 2024

John Michael Smith, 69, of Springfield, passed away peacefully on December 10, 2024, surrounded by his loving family after a courageous battle with cancer.

John was born on March 15, 1955, in Chicago, Illinois, to Robert and Mary Smith. He graduated from Springfield High School in 1973 and went on to earn a Bachelor's degree in Engineering from the University of Illinois.

John dedicated 35 years of his career to ABC Manufacturing, where he worked as a Senior Engineer and was beloved by his colleagues for his kindness and expertise. He was known for his innovative solutions and mentorship of younger engineers.

In 1978, John married his high school sweetheart, Susan Johnson. Together they built a beautiful life and raised three wonderful children: Michael (45), Sarah (42), and David (38).

John was an avid fisherman and loved spending weekends at the lake with his family. He was also passionate about woodworking and created many beautiful pieces for his home and as gifts for loved ones. John was a devoted member of First Baptist Church, where he served as a deacon for over 20 years.

John is survived by his loving wife of 46 years, Susan; his children Michael (wife Jennifer), Sarah (husband Mark), and David (wife Lisa); eight grandchildren: Emma, Jack, Sophie, Lucas, Olivia, Noah, Grace, and Ethan; his sister Patricia Williams; and numerous nieces, nephews, and dear friends.

He was preceded in death by his parents, Robert and Mary Smith, and his brother James Smith.

A celebration of John's life will be held on Saturday, December 14, 2024, at 2:00 PM at First Baptist Church, 123 Main Street, Springfield. Visitation will be from 12:00 PM to 2:00 PM prior to the service.

In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations be made to the American Cancer Society in John's memory.

John's legacy of love, kindness, and dedication to family will live on in the hearts of all who knew him. He will be deeply missed but never forgotten.
