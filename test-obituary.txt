John Smith Obituary

John Michael Smith, 72, of Springfield, passed away peacefully on December 15, 2024, surrounded by his loving family at Springfield General Hospital.

Born on March 3, 1952, in Chicago, Illinois, John was the son of Robert and Mary Smith. He graduated from Springfield High School in 1970 and went on to earn his Bachelor's degree in Engineering from the University of Illinois.

John worked for 35 years as a mechanical engineer at ABC Manufacturing, where he was respected by colleagues and clients alike. He was known for his dedication, problem-solving skills, and mentorship of younger engineers.

He married his beloved wife, Susan, on June 20, 1975. Together they raised three wonderful children: Michael, Sarah, and Jennifer. John was also a devoted grandfather to six grandchildren who brought him immense joy.

John had a passion for woodworking and spent countless hours in his garage workshop creating beautiful furniture for family and friends. He also enjoyed fishing, camping, and coaching little league baseball for over 20 years.

He is survived by his wife Susan; children Michael (Lisa), Sarah (David), and Jennifer (Mark); grandchildren Emma, Tyler, Olivia, Mason, Grace, and Luke; and his sister Patricia Williams.

A memorial service will be held on December 20, 2024, at 2:00 PM at Springfield Community Church. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations be made to the Springfield Youth Baseball League.

John's legacy of kindness, hard work, and dedication to family will live on in the hearts of all who knew him.
